# Core Framework

## Definition
Plasticity is not growth.  
It is susceptibility to change.

## Levels
- Macro: major shocks (trauma, war, parenthood)
- Meso: structured inputs (habits, education, systems)
- Micro: short-lived states (sleep, emotion, novelty)

## Core Principle
Systems do not change uniformly.  
Plasticity emerges unevenly.

## Working Thesis
To understand change, observe systems when they are destabilized—but not yet reorganized.
